Frequently asked questions

/01/ Is stem cell therapy safe? Is regenerative medicine safe?

After aesthetic stem cell injections, post-care is simple but important to support healing and optimize results. Stay hydrated and avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours post-treatment. Skip makeup, saunas, and intense workouts for 24-48 hours to reduce infection risk. Wear sun protection for several months following facial microneedling. Your provider will recommend a personal plan based on your treatment type and needs.

/02/ How long does it take to see results from joint stem cell therapy?

It can take 8 to 12 weeks or more to see results from stem cell therapy for joint pain. Results take place gradually. Unfortunately, some individuals do not respond to therapy.

/03/ Do you offer support for my condition?

We recommend starting the eligibility and consultation process to determine if we can help support your personal health needs.

/04/ Is stem cell therapy and regenerative medicine effective?

Stem cell therapy and regenerative medicine can be very effective. Although some people don't respond to therapy, those who do frequently describe the benefits as less pain, improved daily function, and better quality of life.
